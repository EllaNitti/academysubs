How to encapsulate an object:
<   >

How to express object type:
<type>

How to express and separate attributes, with and without values:
list attributes after type.
encapsulate with ( and )
assign value with =
separate with |

<type(attr1=value|attr2=value|attr33)>

how to express children:
encapsulate children with _ and _
separate children with |

<type(attr1)_<childType(attr)>|<childType(attr)>_>
