Encapsulate an object with {}
Attibutes within ()
Separate attributes and values with =
Separate attributes with |
Children within <>
Separate children with |

Example with a family tree:
Valid attributes: 	age (have value)
			Superpower (does not have value)

{Grandpa(age=80|Paralysed)<{Dad(age=42|Giant)<{Me(age=22|Ninja)}|{Brother1(age=20|Animal)}|{Brother2(age=15|Telepathy)}>}|{Auntie(age=40|Naggy)<{Cousin1(age=13|Retarded)}|{Cousin2(age=11|Retarded^2)}>}>}
